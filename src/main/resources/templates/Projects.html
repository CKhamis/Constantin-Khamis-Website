Project History

Welcome to the Projects page of this website. Here, you'll explore my projects organized into distinct "eras." Each era brings together projects that share common themes and intentions. These eras include the Interdimensional era, Pseudo-Industrial era, High School era, and Childhood era. As we move beyond high school, the era names take on more abstract forms, adding an extra layer of intrigue to the categorization.

My primary focus in coding has predominantly shifted towards the development of full-stack web-based applications. Over the years, I've become well-versed in using Java Spring and Java Hibernate for many of my most recent projects. Additionally, I've also been delving into new frameworks and libraries such as Python Fast and React.

Please note that some of the repositories associated with these projects may be unavailable due to their status as school projects or containing personal information.
